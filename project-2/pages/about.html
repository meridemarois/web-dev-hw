About My Project

The best comfort food of all time: mac & cheese. It's my favorite food when I'm feeling overwhelmed. Here's how I make it.

Thing 1

First, boil a box of elbow macaroni for about seven to eight minutes. When the time is up, drain the macaroni from the water and set aside.

Thing 2

Next, make a roux. Add equal parts butter and flour, and stir with a wooden spoon. The mixture will thicken and turn a pale yellow color. Whisk in milk until a thick sauce forms. Add in cheese, salt, pepper, and mustard powder..

Thing 3

Add the macaroni to the sauce. Some people prefer the macaroni to be transferred to a 9 x 13 pan, top with breadcrumbs, and bake for five minutes. This is optional. Once perfected, serve and enjoy.

Suggested Materials

All that is required for this recipe is a large sauce pan, a wooden spoon, a whisk, and a strainer. For ingredients, you need macaroni, butter, flour, milk, cheese, salt, pepper, and mustard powder.

This is a video helpful in learning how to make a roux. They can be challenging, but they're very useful to know how to do if you're someone who likes to cook.
